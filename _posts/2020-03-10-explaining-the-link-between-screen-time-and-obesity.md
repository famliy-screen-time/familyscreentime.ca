A large amount of research has established a clear link between screen usage and obesity in children. What exactly is behind this link? Do parents need to prohibit screen usage to prevent child obesity, 



In 2017, an international non-profit organization called **Children and Screens: Institute of Digital Media and Child Development** recently released a compilation of recent research about the relationship between screen usage and child obesity, based on an article by the [American Academy of Pediatrics. ](https://pediatrics.aappublications.org/content/pediatrics/140/Supplement_2/S97.full.pdf)



The three important mechanisms of this relationships are:

* Increased eating while viewing screens
* Seeing advertisements for unhealthy foods that alter the child's preferences
* Disrupting sleep
